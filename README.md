### Selection Rationale

Both products were selected over the original ProfEdgeAI affiliate blog concept because:

- **No Google/SEO dependency** — revenue from day one through direct channels
- **Direct payment mechanism** — subscriptions and retainers, not affiliate commissions
- **AI IS the product** — not the content generator behind the product
- **Synergistic ICP** — the same solopreneur/consultant buys both
- **Combined ARPU** — a fully-activated client pays ~$220/month ($20 Sentinel + $199 Ghost)
